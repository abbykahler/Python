Ankle Jumping Jack

60 secs

{% comment %}

Place a mini band loop around your ankles. Stand in a quarter-squat position (a shallow squat), with your feet about hip-width apart and your hands at your chest. Jump your feet out and in for 1 rep, keeping a bent knee position throughout and landing softly each time.
